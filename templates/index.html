Dwarkadas J. Sanghvi College of Engineering

Shri Vile Parle Kelavani Mandal’s (SVKM) Dwarkadas J. Sanghvi College of Engineering (DJSCE), was established in the year 1994. In the span of 27 years, DJSCE has come a long way and has made its impact felt not only in the country, but also abroad. DJSCE is an Autonomous Institution, granted autonomy by the University Grants Commission (UGC) for a period of 10 years, starting from the A.Y. 2019-20 till 2028-29.

Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute

Established in 1887 as Victoria Jubilee Technical Institute, VJTI Mumbai has pioneered India’s Engineering education, research and training ecosystem and has been shifting itself from a training institute to capacity building institute with focus on research, innovation, and technology for society.
